The Magnus Archives

The Magnus Archives is a horror fiction podcast. It is constructed as a series of statements recorded by Jonathan Sims within the Magnus Institute. It is a fictional institute based in London centered on research into the paranormal. The story involves dozens of unique characters all with their respective voice actors.

The podcast has 5 seasons. All of which contain 50 episodes - resulting in 200 episodes total. This marks the end of Magnus Archives. Although there may be a sequel called The Magnus Protocol with different protagonists.

Lore

They are various aspects of an amorphous force of fear that exists next to our reality. They are variously also referred to as Gods, powers, or simply as the Fears. Their influence upon reality manifests as supernatural happenings — all supernatural phenomena in the world are simply extensions of them. These phenomena can take various forms such as people, animals, monsters, books, objects, or places, all with the goal of evoking fear, terror, and paranoia from all who encounter them.

These entities do not simply feed off of fear but are fears made manifest. It is not only human fear that counts but that of animals as well, particularly for The Flesh and The Hunt. The more fearful the world is of a certain thing, the more powerful the related entity becomes, becoming empowered by the increased fear of its realm of influence.

They have been divided by Robert Smirk into 14 main categories. These are as follows:

  1. The Buried - fear of small spaces
  2. The Corruption - fear of desease, filth
  3. The Dark - fear of the dark, of what lies beyond that we cannot see
  4. The Desolation - fear of pain, loss, burning, and destruction especially with senseless cause
  5. The End - fear of death itself- uncaring and inevitable
  6. The Eye - fear of being watched, followed, having secrets exposed
  7. The Flesh - fear held by animals of being bred for meat, and in the human realisation- that we are just animated meat and bones
  8. The Hunt - the animalistic fear of being chased or hunted; fear of being prey
  9. The Lonely - fear of isolation, of being completely cut off and alone or disconnected from society
  10. The Slaughter - fear of pure, unpredictable, unmotivated violence; fear pain coming at sudden, random moments
  11. The Spiral - fear of madness, that the world you know is wrong, that your mind is lying to you; fear of deception, lying, deceiving of mind and senses
  12. The Stranger - the fear of the unknown, the unfamiliar; the creeping sense that something is not right
  13. The Vast - the fear of heights, falling, and large open spaces including sky, space, and deep water; more broadly: the human fear of insignificance and meaninglessness
  14. The Web - the fear of being controlled or trapped, especially being unaware of one´s own entrapment; the fear of being forced to do things against one´s own will, of being manipulated; also the fear of spiders
