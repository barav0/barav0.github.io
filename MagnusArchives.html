The Magnus Archives

The Magnus Archives is a horror fiction podcast. It is constructed as a series of statements recorded by Jonathan Sims within the Magnus Institute, who is the newly appointed archivist. The Magnus institute is a fictional institute based in London centered on research into the paranormal.

The podcast has 5 seasons. All of which contain 50 episodes - resulting in 200 episodes total. This marks the end of Magnus Archives. Although there may be a sequel with different characters.
