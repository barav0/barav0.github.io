The Magnus Archives

The Magnus Archives is a horror fiction podcast. It is constructed as a series of statements recorded by Jonathan Sims within the Magnus Institute, who is the newly appointed archivist. The Magnus institute is a fictional institute based in London centered on research into the paranormal. The story involves dozens of characters all with their respective voice actors.

The podcast has 5 seasons. All of which contain 50 episodes - resulting in 200 episodes total. This marks the end of Magnus Archives. Although there may be a sequel with different characters.

Lore

They are various aspects of an amorphous force of fear that exists next to our reality. They are variously also referred to as Gods, powers, or simply as the Fears. Their influence upon reality manifests as supernatural happenings — all supernatural phenomena in the world are simply extensions of them. These phenomena can take various forms such as people, animals, monsters, books, objects, or places, all with the goal of evoking fear, terror, and paranoia from all who encounter them.
